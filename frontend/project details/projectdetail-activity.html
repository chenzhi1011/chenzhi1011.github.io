Renovation and Expansion of the Temple

Design background:

The goal is to preserve the temple in the area and use it as a catalyst to activate community activities, drawing people together and fostering a sense of connection.

Design:

The introduction of new materials transforms sightlines and spatial perceptions. This design investigates the movement patterns of people using the temple and the potential activities around it. By analyzing the daily routines of local residents and studying typical spaces in the area, the project integrates these findings with innovative materials to create a rural community center that blends tradition with modernity.
