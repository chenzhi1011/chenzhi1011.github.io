Framing Kawada

Details

Try to use wooden material for construction.(In Sabae,Japan)

Design background:

The village where this project is located boasts incredibly beautiful scenery. From the window or door of any household in the village, one can enjoy such views. This inspired our concept--Framing

At the same time, it also carries the meaning of preserving the beautiful scenery.

Design

According to our concept, we organized our structure using multiple overlapping frames. This approach takes inspiration from the local method of timber joinery. Additionally, the depth created by the overlapping frames helps guide people's line of sight.

This is an installation where people can rest, play, and enjoy the scenery.
